Daycare Features

Outdoor Play

We believe it outdoor play and excersize and encourage healthy fitness habits.

  • Learn how to share toys and play together.
  • Connect with nature and release energy.
  • Learn movment and coordination through outdoor play.
  • Use creativity to engage with the environment.
  • Develop friendships and avoid screen time.

Learning Activities

Children are engaged in learning activities including the following.

  • Circle time.
  • Theater puppet shows.
  • Daily reading time.
  • Introduction to colors and numbers.
  • Memory games using shapes and animals.

Let the Creativity Flow

We believe that children need to let their creative insitincts flourish.

  • Arts and crafts time.
  • Drawing and painting activiites.
  • Music lessons.
  • Dance circles and creative movement.
