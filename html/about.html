About Us

Daycare Overview

Children's Garden Home Daycare prides itself on its small classroom size with a max of six children. This allows us to focus on the individual needs of our students to ensure they can build the early developement skills they will need to flourish in life. We avoid screen time and focus on human interaction, interactive outdoor games, and incorporate daily learning activiities in our curriculum.


Our Founder


Marcella

Marcella founded Children's Garden out of her love for teaching children. Following a successful career as a lawyer in Brazil, Marcella discovered her passion for child developement and has received her Certificates in Early Development and Nutrition. Marcella's focus in on giving the children of her care pursonalized attention to guarantee that they learn and flourish.
